Our mission
Datameer's customers need to perform various business critical analytical workloads based on many large data sets on premise or in the cloud. This includes classic Data Preparation, Business Intelligence as well as ML/AI tasks. Therefore, they invest into dedicated compute resources. Our goal is to build software that enables our customers to run this analytics, leveraging their resources in the most effective way. This incorporates the integration of Datameer solutions into a larger IT ecosystem as well as the translation and execution of those analytical tasks to Spark or cloud vendor specific execution engines.
Therefore, we need your skills.
