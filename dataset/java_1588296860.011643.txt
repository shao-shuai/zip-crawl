Title: Staff Technical Writer - w/Java Coding
Company: Salesforce.com Inc

Type: Full-Time

Job Details
Job Details
MuleSoft is looking for an experienced, talented, and motivated Senior Technical Writer to produce high-quality technical content for developers or DevOps engineers. You know how to work collaboratively with other writers, Product Management, Engineering, customers, and others to create the technical topics and examples that customers can find on Google.
Phrases such as "topic-based," "Google-ready," "developer-focused," "task-oriented," and "component documentation" flow naturally whenever you talk about your work. You are unafraid to ask questions because you love to learn about complex technologies. If you are a problem-solver who can deliver quality content in a demanding environment under tight deadlines, then this may be your perfect opportunity to take your career to the next level on a small, agile writing team at a fast-growing company.
3 Months
What you'll achieve:
You understand all of the core use cases for one or more technologies and are able to
thoroughly document them
You are self-directed in working with engineering to gather doc requirements for
upcoming releases, proactively responding to feedback about documentation for your
focused technology or technologies
You understand our other technologies well enough to write topics for larger team
deliverables and have developed a strong relationship with Engineering to manage
successful tech review of new or existing content
12 Months
You demonstrate expertise in one or more of MuleSoft's technologies and know how
our customers use them
You have a reputation for writing trusted guidance and have built a strong working
relationship with Engineering stakeholders to draft, review, and publish content
You proactively respond to and seek documentation feedback from internal and
external customers
You have evaluated one or more documentation sets, implement organizational and
structural changes, and taken ownership of the process of peer and technical reviews
of your content
What You'll Need To Be Successful
Ability to read and, ideally, write code in Java or a scripting language
Experience documenting APIs, SDKs, and cloud-based enterprise software
Experience with Git, Markdown, and working from the command line required
Experience with DITA and optimizing content for Google is a plus
Ability to break down problems
Exceptional writing skills with a strong voice and sharp eye for structure, along with the
subject-matter knowledge required for credibility
Strong understanding of agile software development
